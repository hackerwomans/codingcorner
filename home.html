I'M KEANNA VO. I LIKE ART , DOGSAND WATCHING:

I study Graphic Design and I am learning the art of coding. Good ol' HTML, CSS and JavaScript are no longer strangers to me, instead, they are growing to become very close friends. We all have tea with each other on Sundays during brunch.

This site was inspired by the aesthetics of brutalist web design. The goal was to keep the content light-hearted, optimistic and true to who I am. I like memes and breakfast foods. An important thing for me while creating this site was to include requirements from the brief in creative and fun ways. There are 5 images from a folder used as my likes and the open/close arrows, while my external images are being used as the site's cursors. As for the numbered list, a delicious instruction how-to.
